# SentQuote Organic Growth Operations (0-100 Customers)

## Goal and Constraints
- Goal: first 100 paying customers using organic channels only.
- Constraint: no ads, no automation spam, no fake social proof.
- Positioning: not proposal software, not CRM, not invoicing. SentQuote is send, track, get paid.

## ICP and Message Anchor
- ICP: solo operators and 1-3 person service shops sending 5-20 quotes/month.
- Core pain: "client went dark after quote."
- Anchor line: "Send quotes. Know when they're read. Get paid."

## Weekly Execution Rhythm
1. Monday: review prior week metrics, keep top 2 message angles, rewrite the bottom 2.
2. Tuesday-Thursday (9-11 AM EST): publish 1 Reddit post/day in different subreddits and reply to every comment within 2-6 hours.
3. Friday: publish 2 X posts (insight + build-in-public) and make 1 high-value community contribution without hard selling.
4. Daily (30-45 min): search X for pain-intent posts and send up to 10 personalized one-touch replies/messages.

## Channel Operating Rules
- Reddit: story first, no link-first posts, transparent "I built this."
- X: reply to in-market pain, short and direct, no generic founder content.
- Communities: contribute 3-5 days before product mention.
- Posting cap: one post per subreddit per week.

## 30-Day Output Targets
- Reddit posts: 10
- X pain replies: 80-120
- Community contributions: 20+
- Blog posts: 2
- Testimonial asks: after first 10 active users

## Message Iteration Loop
1. Log post angle, persona, and hook for every post.
2. Tag replies by objection type (price, alternatives, trust, complexity).
3. Weekly: keep top 2 angles, cut bottom 2, rewrite and retest.
4. Double down on persona with highest quote-sent activation.
